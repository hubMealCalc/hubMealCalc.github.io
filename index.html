A Guide to Hub Meal Calculator

Creator: Olivia Mallon (contact: oliviamallon2020@gmail.com)

This is a calculator to help calculate shared meal costs among housemates.

Step by Step Guide

  1. Enter in the number of housemates you have
  2. You will then be prompted to enter in the names of all your housemates
  3. Enter in the number of weeks you would like to calculate
  4. There will be x number of tables generated (x being the number of weeks you want to calculate)
  5. Fill in the table, with the cost of each meal, chef (the person who cooked the meal), select all present (if all your housemates were at the meal), present: which housemates were at the meal.
  6. Once all the information is filled in, click the "Submit" button and a table will appear with costs
  7. OWED - meals this person is owed money by other people, OWES - this person owes people money
